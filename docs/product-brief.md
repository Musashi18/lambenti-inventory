# Product Brief

## Product

**Lambenti** is a magnetic light interaction lighting system. A user can trigger ambient lighting by moving a magnetic object on their desk, creating a tactile interaction between physical movement and light.

## Business Need

As Lambenti moves from prototype toward repeatable production, material tracking becomes critical. The stock management system should reduce build delays, clarify purchasing needs, and create the foundation for agent-assisted procurement.

## Operational Context

Likely inventory categories include:

- electronic components
- magnets and sensing hardware
- housings and mechanical parts
- lighting elements
- assembly consumables
- packaging

The system should support both:

- **development mode**, where quantities are small and parts change frequently
- **production mode**, where bills of materials, suppliers, and reorder processes become more stable

## Success Criteria

- Team can see available stock for every critical part.
- Upcoming shortages are visible before they interrupt builds.
- Supplier and lead-time information is centralized.
- Purchase orders can be drafted quickly and reviewed confidently.
- Future agents can operate on structured data rather than informal notes.
