Lambenti concept test

Q23. Based on this description, how interested are you in this type of product?

Imagine a plug-in and stick-on ambient lighting product for a desk, shelf, or TV stand. It sits underneath the visible surface, out-of-sight, and controls a warm or cool white backlight. Instead of using a visible switch, remote, or app, you adjust the brightness by moving a small magnetic object on the surface above it. Visualize a wooden hexagon the size of a large coin with a velvet bottom.
